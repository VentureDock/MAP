Nurture 2 — Value Email

Nurture · Day 5
Subject: The #1 mistake international founders make in the US (and how to fix it)  |  Trigger: 5 days after Nurture 1 (if no reply / no application)

VentureDock

The #1 mistake: entering the US with your home-market ICP

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

Of all the ways we've seen international founders stumble in the US, this one hurts the most — because it wastes the most time and money before you realize the problem:

"They assumed their best home-market customer type would be their best US customer type. It almost never is."

Why? US buyers have different:

  • Risk tolerance (they need proof of outcome, not proof of concept)
  • Buying processes (procurement, legal, infosec review — all real)
  • Competitive reference points (they're comparing you to US-native alternatives you may not know)
  • Success metrics (their definition of ROI may be entirely different from your home market)

The fix: define your US ICP from scratch. Don't port your existing one over. This is the first thing we do in VentureDock — and it changes everything downstream (GTM, messaging, hiring, fundraising).
